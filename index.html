Revenge of the Octocat

This was my project for the first term of AP Computer Science (Junior year of high school). Written in Java Swing, this is a top-down shooter game where the main character is the Github mascot: the Octocat!

The story: The Github servers have been bugged up! Play as the Octocat, arm yourself with lots of \xDE\xAD\xBE\xEF to shoot into those pesky bugs and make them all go away!

Features:

  1. Boss levels on stages 11, 21, and 31
  2. Background music by Waterflame89
  3. Lots of Github references!
  4. Boss AI

About Me

I am currently a junior attending Stuyvesant High School in New York City. My main interests are computer science, robotics, mathematics, and 3D modelling. I enjoy doing cyber security CTF competitions such as picoCTF, and am currently affiliated with teams StuyFyreCS and in/s/ane. My first CTF was picoCTF 2014, where my team and I peaked at 51st place and ended 78th place (StuyFyreCS).

I also enjoy using IRC (irc.freenode.net:6667, channel #stuyfyre) along with friends (I also discovered IRC through CTFs). My friend Yicheng Wang wrote an IRC bot for our private channel.
